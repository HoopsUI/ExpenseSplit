Common Questions About Splitting Utilities Between Roommates

How do roommates split electricity bills fairly?

Electricity bills can be split equally or based on usage. If one roommate uses AC, heaters, or works from home, a usage-based split is more fair. Otherwise, equal splitting works for similar lifestyles.

Should WiFi bills be split equally?

Yes, WiFi is usually split equally since all roommates typically have access. However, if one person uses significantly more bandwidth (gaming, streaming, work), you may adjust the split.

What if one roommate uses more utilities?

If one roommate consumes more electricity or water, it’s fair to adjust their share. You can either estimate usage or track expenses and settle balances using a calculator.

What is the best way to split utility bills?

The best method is to track who paid each bill and calculate balances at the end. This avoids constant calculations and ensures fairness.
