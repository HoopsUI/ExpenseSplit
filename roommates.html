Why Use Our Roommates Expense Calculator?

Managing shared expenses among roommates can get messy. From rent to utilities to groceries, manually calculating each person's share often leads to errors, disputes, or delays. ExpenseSplit simplifies everything:

  • Quickly add roommates and shared expenses.
  • Automatic calculation of who owes whom.
  • Track multiple expenses in one place.
  • Zero signup required, fast and easy.

Easy to Use

Simply enter the number of roommates, their names, expenses, who paid, and who shared. Click calculate and get instant results. It’s like having a mini Splitwise right in your browser!
