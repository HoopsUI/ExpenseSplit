How to Split Expenses Fairly

When dividing shared expenses, there are several methods you can use.

1. Equal Split : Everyone pays the same amount.

Best for:

  • dinners
  • taxis
  • group activities

2. Proportional Split : Expenses are divided based on income.

Best for:

  • rent
  • shared living costs
  • long-term partnerships

3. Item-Based Split : Each person pays only for what they ordered or used.

Best for:

  • restaurant bills
  • groceries
  • shopping
