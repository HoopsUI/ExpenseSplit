Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains how ExpenseSplit handles information when you use our website and tools.

ExpenseSplit is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that are collected and recorded by ExpenseSplit and how we use it.

Information We Do Not Collect

ExpenseSplit does not require users to create accounts, sign in, or provide personal details such as names, phone numbers, or payment information. The expense calculations you perform remain entirely within your browser and are not stored on our servers.

Information We May Collect

Like many websites, ExpenseSplit may collect non-personal information automatically, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • General geographic location (country or region)

This information is used only to understand how visitors use the site and to improve performance, usability, and content quality.

Cookies and Web Beacons

ExpenseSplit may use cookies to store information about visitors’ preferences and optimize the website experience. Cookies help us understand which pages are most useful and how users interact with the site.

Google Analytics

ExpenseSplit uses Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and report information about website usage.

The data collected by Google Analytics is aggregated and anonymous. You can learn how Google uses data collected from sites that use its services by visiting Google’s official privacy documentation.

Google AdSense

In the future, ExpenseSplit may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies, including the DoubleClick cookie, to show ads to users based on their visits to this and other websites.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google’s Ads Settings.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

ExpenseSplit’s Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of third-party ad servers or websites for more detailed information about their practices.

Children’s Information

ExpenseSplit does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has provided personal information on this website, please contact us immediately and we will promptly remove such information.

Consent

By using ExpenseSplit, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated policy will be effective immediately upon posting.
