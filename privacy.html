Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains how ExpenseSplit handles information when you use our website and tools.

ExpenseSplit is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that are collected and recorded by ExpenseSplit and how we use it.

How ExpenseSplit Tools Handle Your Data

ExpenseSplit is designed around simple browser-based calculators. When you use tools such as the Airbnb Cost Split Calculator, Gas Cost Split Calculator, Roommate Expense Calculator, Monthly Expense Tracker, or other budgeting tools, the information you enter is processed directly within your browser.

ExpenseSplit does not require account creation and does not store your expense entries, bill amounts, roommate costs, trip budgets, subscription costs, or other calculator inputs on our servers.

Once you leave the page or clear your browser data, your calculations are no longer available unless you saved them yourself.

Our Privacy Philosophy

ExpenseSplit was built to help people split expenses, track budgets, and manage shared costs without requiring account registration or unnecessary personal information.

Many finance-related tools require sign-ups before users can access basic calculations. ExpenseSplit aims to keep simple budgeting and expense-sharing tools accessible without forcing users to create accounts or share personal details.
