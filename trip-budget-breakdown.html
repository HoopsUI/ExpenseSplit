Trip Budget Formula (Cost Per Person)

To calculate your trip budget per person, use this simple travel cost formula:

  • Total Trip Cost ÷ Number of People = Cost Per Person

Trip Budget Examples for Different Scenarios

The total cost of a trip depends on the duration, destination, and travel style. Below are some common trip scenarios to help you estimate your budget.

1. Weekend Trip (2–3 Days)

  • Budget: ₹5,000 – ₹12,000 per person
  • Includes short travel, budget hotel, and basic meals
  • Best for quick getaways with friends

2. 5–7 Day Trip

  • Budget: ₹15,000 – ₹35,000 per person
  • Includes flights/train, hotel stay, food, and activities
  • Ideal for vacations or group travel

3. Solo Trip

  • Higher per-person cost since expenses are not shared
  • More flexibility but less cost efficiency

4. Group Trip (4–6 People)

  • Lower cost per person due to shared expenses
  • Best for splitting hotel, transport, and activities
