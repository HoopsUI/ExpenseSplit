Common Ways to Split Bills With Friends

1. Equal Split (Split Bill Evenly)

The simplest method is splitting the total bill equally between everyone.

If the total bill is $120 and four people are sharing the cost, each person pays $30.

2. Itemized Split (Split Bill Based on What You Ordered)

Sometimes people order different things or spend different amounts. In that case, splitting the bill equally may not feel fair.

An itemized split assigns each person only the expenses they were responsible for.

3. Percentage Split (Uneven Expense Splitting)

Another option is splitting the bill based on percentages.

What Is the Best Way to Split Bills With Friends?

The best way to split bills depends on the situation. While equal splitting works in simple cases, it may not always be fair when people spend different amounts.

  • Equal split: Best for shared experiences like taxis or rent
  • Itemized split: Ideal for restaurant bills
  • Proportional split: Useful for trips or uneven contributions
