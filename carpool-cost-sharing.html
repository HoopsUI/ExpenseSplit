How to Split Gas Costs in a Carpool

There are several ways to divide fuel expenses depending on your situation:

1. Equal Split

Divide the total fuel cost equally among all passengers. This is the simplest and most common method.

2. Split by Distance

If passengers join or leave at different points, costs can be divided based on distance traveled.

3. Driver Pays Less (Optional)

In some carpools, passengers cover a larger portion to compensate the driver for wear and tear on the vehicle.
