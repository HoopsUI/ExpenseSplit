About ExpenseSplit

A simple way to split expenses — no accounts, no confusion

What is ExpenseSplit?

ExpenseSplit is a free online expense split calculator designed to help people quickly and fairly divide shared expenses. Whether you're traveling with friends, living with roommates, or managing group outings, ExpenseSplit makes it easy to see who owes whom — without creating accounts or installing apps.

The idea behind ExpenseSplit is simplicity. Many expense tracking tools are overloaded with features, logins, and complex workflows. ExpenseSplit focuses only on what most people actually need: a fast and clear way to split expenses and settle balances.

Why ExpenseSplit Exists

Splitting expenses shouldn’t be stressful. Small misunderstandings about money can easily turn into awkward conversations or disputes. ExpenseSplit was created to remove that friction by offering a transparent and easy-to-use tool that anyone can understand.

There is no registration, no personal data collection, and no complicated setup. You open the site, enter the expenses, and get the results instantly.

Who Can Use ExpenseSplit?

ExpenseSplit is useful for a wide range of everyday situations, including:

  • Friends splitting food, movies, or outings
  • Roommates sharing rent, utilities, and groceries
  • Travel groups managing trip expenses
  • Students managing shared costs
  • Anyone who wants a quick expense breakdown

The calculator works smoothly on both desktop and mobile devices, making it accessible wherever you are.

Free and Easy to Use

ExpenseSplit is completely free to use. There is no signup required, no account creation, and no hidden paywalls for the basic calculator. You can start using the tool immediately without sharing any personal information.

In the future, additional advanced features may be introduced, but the basic expense splitting functionality will always remain simple and accessible.

Our Goal

The goal of ExpenseSplit is to provide a reliable, lightweight, and user-friendly expense calculator that anyone can use without friction. We aim to keep the platform clean, fast, and focused on solving real-world problems.

We continuously work on improving usability, clarity, and performance while keeping the experience distraction-free.

Get Started

You can start using ExpenseSplit right away by visiting the home page and entering your expenses. No downloads, no signups — just simple expense splitting.
