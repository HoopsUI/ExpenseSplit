Is It Legal to Share Netflix or Spotify Accounts?

Yes — but only under specific conditions.

  • Netflix: Allows sharing within the same household.
  • Spotify Family: Requires users to live at the same address.
  • Other services: Have different rules depending on their terms.

Sharing accounts outside allowed limits may result in:

  • Account restrictions
  • Extra charges
  • Account suspension
