How the IOU Debt Simplifier Works

Our calculator uses a debt simplification algorithm similar to the one used in popular expense apps. The goal is to reduce the number of transactions required to settle group expenses. For example, consider this situation:

  • Alice owes Bob $20
  • Bob owes Charlie $20

Instead of making two payments, the calculator simplifies the debt to:

Alice pays Charlie $20

This process is called debt simplification or transaction minimization. It helps groups settle expenses quickly without unnecessary payments between multiple people.
