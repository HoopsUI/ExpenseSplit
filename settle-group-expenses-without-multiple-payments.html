How Debt Simplification Works (The Logic Behind Fewer Payments)

One of the biggest problems in group expense settlement is unnecessary transactions. Without simplification, everyone may end up paying everyone else — creating a confusing web of payments.

The goal is to minimize group payments by reducing all balances into the smallest number of transactions possible.

Basic Idea of Expense Settlement Algorithm

  • Calculate how much each person paid
  • Calculate how much each person should pay
  • Find the difference (who owes vs who gets)
  • Match debtors with creditors
  • Reduce payments to minimum transfers

This method is often called a group expense settlement algorithm and is used by modern expense split tools.
