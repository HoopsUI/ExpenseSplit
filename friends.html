How to Split Bills Between Friends Fairly

Many groups struggle with one simple question: Should expenses be split equally or based on who paid? The answer depends on the situation.

If everyone consumed the same things (for example a shared dinner or taxi ride), an equal bill split is usually the simplest approach. However, if some friends paid for larger items such as hotel bookings or event tickets, it's often better to track individual payments and calculate balances.
