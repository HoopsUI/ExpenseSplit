Why Use Our Friends Expense Calculator?

Sharing costs with friends can get confusing fast. Whether it’s a dinner, trip, or event, manually figuring out who owes what leads to mistakes and awkward conversations. ExpenseSplit simplifies the process:

  • Add friends and shared expenses quickly.
  • Automatically calculate each person’s share.
  • Keep a running total for multiple expenses.
  • No login needed — just open and use!

Easy to Use

Simply enter the total expense amount and number of friends. ExpenseSplit instantly shows how much each person needs to pay, making group expenses simple and transparent.

Perfect for:

  • Group dinners or outings.
  • Friends sharing travel costs.
  • Event planning and party expenses.
