Without a clear system, one person often ends up paying more than their fair share.

Best Ways to Split Subscription Costs

1. Equal Split (Most Common)

Divide the total subscription price equally among all users.

Example: ₹800 Netflix plan shared by 4 people → each pays ₹200.

2. Per-User Pricing Model

Some subscriptions charge based on number of users or profiles. In this case, each user pays only for their slot.

3. Usage-Based Splitting

If some users use the service more than others, costs can be split proportionally.

4. Fixed Owner + Contributors

One person owns the subscription and others contribute a fixed amount monthly.
