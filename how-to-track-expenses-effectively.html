50/30/20 Rule Explained (Simple Budgeting Method)

One of the most popular budgeting strategies is the 50/30/20 rule. It helps you manage money while tracking expenses effectively.

  • 50% Needs: Rent, groceries, bills
  • 30% Wants: Entertainment, eating out
  • 20% Savings: Investments, emergency fund

For example, if your monthly income is ₹50,000:

  • ₹25,000 → Needs
  • ₹15,000 → Wants
  • ₹10,000 → Savings

By combining this rule with a daily expense tracker, you can clearly see whether your spending matches your budget.
