How to Split Expenses with ExpenseSplit

Quickly and fairly divide group expenses without any hassle. Perfect for trips, roommates, and friends.

Why Split Expenses?

Splitting expenses fairly is essential for avoiding confusion and disagreements among friends, family, or roommates. Whether you are planning a group trip, sharing rent, or tracking everyday costs with friends, ExpenseSplit makes it simple and efficient.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using ExpenseSplit

Follow these easy steps to start splitting expenses effortlessly:

  1. Open the Calculator: Go to the main calculator or choose a specific calculator like Trip Calculator, Roommates Calculator, or Friends Calculator.
  2. Add Participants: Enter the names of everyone sharing the expense. Each participant can be assigned multiple payments.
  3. Enter Expenses: Input the amount paid by each person and the description of the expense. You can add as many expenses as needed.
  4. Calculate Balances: Click “Calculate” to instantly see who owes whom and how much. No signup is required, and calculations are performed right in your browser.
  5. Share Results: You can copy the results or take a screenshot to share with the group.

Tips for Using ExpenseSplit Effectively

  • Enter expenses as soon as they occur to avoid forgetting any payments.
  • Double-check totals for accuracy, especially for group trips with many participants.
  • Use the appropriate calculator depending on your situation (Trip, Roommates, or Friends) for a faster experience.
  • Keep the browser tab open until all balances are calculated, as the data is not stored on the server.

Common Use Cases

ExpenseSplit is perfect for everyday group expenses:

  • Group Trips: Track shared travel, lodging, and food expenses.
  • Roommates: Split rent, utilities, groceries, and other shared costs.
  • Friends & Social Events: Split bills for dinners, parties, or outings effortlessly.
