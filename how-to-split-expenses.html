How to Split Expenses with ExpenseSplit

Quickly and fairly divide group expenses among friends, roommates, or during trips using our free, easy-to-use calculator.

Step 1: Open the Calculator

Go to our main calculator page by clicking here. No signup or login is required — ExpenseSplit works entirely in your browser.

Step 2: Enter Participants

Add the names of all people who are sharing expenses. You can include friends, roommates, or colleagues. Make sure everyone is accounted for so the split is accurate.

Step 3: Add Expenses

Enter each expense, the amount, and select who paid for it. You can also choose which participants share the cost. This allows you to handle unequal payments or shared expenses for specific people.

Step 4: Calculate Splits

Click the “Calculate” button. ExpenseSplit will instantly show how much each person owes or should be reimbursed. You can view totals clearly and use this to settle debts quickly.

Step 5: Tips for Accuracy

  • Double-check amounts before submitting.
  • Use clear participant names to avoid confusion.
  • Enter expenses as they occur for the most accurate results.

Step 6: Share the Results

Once calculated, you can share the results manually or screenshot the summary for the group. This ensures everyone knows their share and helps prevent disagreements.

Why Use ExpenseSplit?

ExpenseSplit is free, fast, and privacy-focused. Unlike other apps, you don’t need to sign up or provide personal details. All calculations happen in your browser, keeping your information safe.
