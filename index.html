💙 Why I Built ExpenseSplit

Hi, and welcome to ExpenseSplit.

ExpenseSplit started from a problem I've seen again and again in everyday life. Whether it was splitting restaurant bills with friends, dividing rent and utilities with roommates, or managing costs during group trips, the same question always seemed to come up: "Who owes whom?"

What should have been a simple calculation often became confusing. Someone paid for dinner, someone else booked the accommodation, another person covered fuel costs, and before long people were scrolling through messages trying to figure everything out.

I created ExpenseSplit because I wanted a simpler solution.

Instead of forcing people to download another app, create accounts, or learn complicated software, I wanted to build practical tools that solve specific expense-sharing problems quickly and clearly.

Today, ExpenseSplit includes calculators and guides for trips, roommates, restaurants, grocery sharing, subscriptions, weddings, and many other situations where people need a fair way to divide costs.

My goal is simple: help people spend less time calculating expenses and more time enjoying the experiences they're sharing.

Thank you for visiting ExpenseSplit and supporting this project.

— Founder, ExpenseSplit

    Yes, the basic calculator is completely free. You can use it as often as you like without any limitations. There are no hidden charges, subscriptions, or intrusive ads, ensuring a smooth user experience.
