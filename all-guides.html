How to Split Expenses Fairly (Step-by-Step)

Splitting expenses fairly depends on the situation. Whether you're sharing bills with friends, roommates, or a group, the goal is to divide costs in a way that feels transparent and fair to everyone involved.

  • List all shared expenses
  • Decide whether to split equally or based on usage
  • Track who paid for what
  • Calculate balances clearly
  • Settle payments with minimum transactions

For example, restaurant bills may be split equally, while rent or travel costs may need custom calculations.
