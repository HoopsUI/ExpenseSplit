Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about using ExpenseSplit to divide expenses fairly and easily.

What is ExpenseSplit?

ExpenseSplit is a free, browser-based expense split calculator that helps groups divide shared costs fairly. It is designed for friends, roommates, trips, and any situation where multiple people share expenses.

Do I need to create an account to use ExpenseSplit?

No. ExpenseSplit does not require any signup, login, or account creation. You can use the calculator instantly without providing personal information.

Is ExpenseSplit really free?

Yes. The basic expense split calculator is completely free to use. There are no hidden charges, subscriptions, or payment requirements.

Does ExpenseSplit store my data?

No. All calculations are performed directly in your browser. ExpenseSplit does not store your expense data, participant names, or results on any server.

Can I split expenses unevenly?

Yes. You can choose who paid for each expense and who should share it. This allows you to handle unequal payments or expenses shared by only certain participants.

Who can use ExpenseSplit?

ExpenseSplit is suitable for:

  • Friends sharing group expenses
  • Roommates splitting rent or utilities
  • Travel groups managing trip costs
  • Colleagues sharing informal expenses

How accurate are the calculations?

ExpenseSplit calculates totals based strictly on the information you enter. For best accuracy, ensure all participants and expenses are entered correctly before calculating.

Can I use ExpenseSplit on mobile devices?

Yes. ExpenseSplit is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers. No app installation is required.

How do I share the results with my group?

You can share results manually or by taking a screenshot of the final summary. This helps everyone clearly understand who owes what.

Is ExpenseSplit similar to Splitwise?

ExpenseSplit offers a simpler, faster alternative. Unlike apps that require accounts and data storage, ExpenseSplit focuses on quick calculations with no login.

Are there advanced features?

ExpenseSplit currently focuses on a simple and reliable basic calculator. Advanced features may be added in the future, but the core tool will remain easy and accessible.
