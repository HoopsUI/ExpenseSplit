Contact ExpenseSplit

Have a question, suggestion, or feedback? We’re always happy to hear from our users.

ExpenseSplit is built to be simple, fast, and accessible for everyone. While the tool is designed to work without the need for support, we understand that users may still have questions, ideas, or concerns.

ExpenseSplit does not use user accounts or support ticket systems. This keeps the platform lightweight and privacy-friendly. Email communication allows us to stay accessible without collecting unnecessary user data.
