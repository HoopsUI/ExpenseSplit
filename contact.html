What User Feedback Has Already Influenced

ExpenseSplit wasn't launched as a finished product. Many of the tools, guides, and improvements on this website exist because visitors took the time to point out confusing areas, missing features, or situations that weren't being covered well enough.

While not every suggestion can be implemented immediately, user feedback regularly helps shape the direction of the website and the tools we build.

Examples of Changes Inspired by Feedback

  • Clearer calculator instructions
    Several visitors mentioned that they understood the final result but weren't always sure what information to enter first. Because of this, many calculators now include additional explanations and examples before the tool itself.
  • More real-life examples
    Some readers found budgeting concepts easier to understand when they could see practical situations rather than definitions. This led to the addition of more scenario-based examples throughout the website.
  • New calculator ideas
    A number of calculators currently available on ExpenseSplit started as suggestions from users looking for ways to handle specific expense sharing situations involving trips, roommates, subscriptions, and group purchases.
  • Improved navigation between tools and guides
    Visitors often wanted an easier way to move from a guide to a calculator and back again. Based on that feedback, additional related links and navigation sections were added across the site.
  • Keeping the website simple
    One of the most common pieces of feedback has been appreciation for tools that work instantly without accounts, downloads, or complicated setup. Maintaining that simplicity continues to influence future updates.

Every message may not receive an immediate response, but every piece of feedback is read and considered. The goal of ExpenseSplit is simple: create tools and resources that solve real-world expense sharing and budgeting problems as clearly as possible.

Who Uses ExpenseSplit?

ExpenseSplit is used by people who simply want a quick way to work out who owes what without downloading an app, creating an account, or sharing personal information.

Some visitors use it to split Airbnb costs with friends, others use it for roommate bills, road trips, shared subscriptions, group dinners, or everyday household expenses. Most are just looking for a straightforward answer when money needs to be divided fairly.

The goal of ExpenseSplit has always been simple: make expense sharing less awkward and easier to understand.
