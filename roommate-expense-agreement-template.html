What is a Roommate Expense Agreement?

A roommate expense agreement is a simple document that outlines how shared expenses will be split between people living together. It removes confusion by defining responsibilities in advance.

  • Who pays rent and when
  • How utilities are divided
  • How shared purchases are handled
  • How to settle payments

This concept is also widely used in group travel situations where multiple people share accommodation, food, and transportation costs.

This type of document is also known as a bill splitting agreement or expense sharing agreement template, commonly used by roommates to avoid disputes.
